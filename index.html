Our Services

Comprehensive post-harvest solutions for agricultural excellence

Sterilization

Advanced sterilization processes eliminate harmful pathogens and contaminants, ensuring your agricultural products meet the highest safety standards for global markets. Our eco-friendly sterilization methods preserve product integrity while extending shelf life.

  • UV-C sterilization technology
  • Ozone treatment systems
  • Hot water treatment
  • Chemical-free options available

Sorting

Precision sorting technology separates good from bad, ensuring only the highest quality products proceed through our facility. Our automated systems use advanced imaging and AI to detect defects, size variations, and quality parameters.

  • Optical sorting technology
  • Size and weight classification
  • Defect detection and removal
  • Quality grading systems

Packing

Professional packing services prepare your products for safe transport and optimal presentation. Our packaging solutions are ready for shipment via plane or ship, designed to maintain product quality during extended transportation periods.

  • Climate-controlled packaging
  • Export-ready containers
  • Custom packaging solutions
  • Biodegradable packaging options

Shipping

Comprehensive shipping coordination ensures your products reach their destination in perfect condition. We manage logistics, documentation, and coordinate with international carriers to provide seamless global distribution services.

  • Global logistics coordination
  • Cold chain management
  • Documentation and certification
  • Real-time tracking systems
